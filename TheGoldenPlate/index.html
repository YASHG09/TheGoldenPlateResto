Chicken Handi

Indulge in the rich, creamy flavor of Chicken Handi, where tender chicken pieces are slow-cooked in a flavorful blend of aromatic spices and a thick, velvety gravy. This dish is perfect for those who love a mix of tradition and indulgence, offering a unique, home-style taste that's both comforting and delicious.

Chicken Tandoori

Our Chicken Tandoori is a smoky, succulent dish marinated in a blend of yogurt and bold spices, then grilled to perfection in a tandoor. The result is a beautifully charred exterior with juicy, tender meat on the inside. Perfect for anyone who enjoys the flavors of traditional Indian grilling.

Chicken Do Pyaza

Chicken Do Pyaza is a flavorful dish where chicken is cooked with two varieties of onions â€“ one in the gravy and one as a garnish. The result is a perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors, with the onions adding a delightful crunch to the dish.

Paneer Tikka Masala

Paneer Tikka Masala is a classic Indian favorite where marinated paneer cubes are grilled and then cooked in a spiced tomato-based gravy. The smoky flavor of the grilled paneer combined with the aromatic sauce makes this dish a must-try for anyone who loves rich, flavorful vegetarian food.

Paneer Butter Masala
